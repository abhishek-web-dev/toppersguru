Admission Policy

Review our enrollment processes, batch distributions, required document verification list, and withdrawal norms.

1. Eligibility Criteria

Admissions to specific programs (NIOS, CBSE, CUET, Skill Courses) are based on the eligibility criteria mandated by the respective educational boards or our internal academic requirements.

2. Documents Required

Students must submit copies of previous mark sheets, Aadhaar Card / ID proof, and passport-size photographs during the admission process for verification.

3. Process & Timeline

Admissions are processed on a first-come, first-served basis. Seat confirmation occurs only after the submission of documents and the payment of the registration fee.

4. Batch Allocation

Batch timings and allocations are decided by management based on seat availability. While we try to accommodate preferences, final allocations are binding.

5. Withdrawal Policy

Cancellations and withdrawals must be formally requested in writing before the commencement of the batch to be considered for any partial refund (see Refund Policy).
