Refund Policy

Understand our guidelines and processing rules regarding fee refunds, cancellations, and course registration changes.

1. General Policy

Toppers Guru maintains a strict no-refund policy once enrollment is confirmed and services (classes or study material) have commenced.

2. Exceptions

Refunds may only be considered in exceptional cases subject to strict management approval. These are evaluated on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

3. Non-Transferable Fees

Fees paid for a specific course or by a specific student are strictly non-transferable to other courses or other individuals.

4. Cancellation Process

If you wish to cancel before the batch starts, a formal written request must be sent to info@toppersguru.in at least 7 days before commencement.

5. Processing Timeline

Approved refunds (pre-commencement only) will be processed within 15-20 business days to the original mode of payment after deducting processing charges.
